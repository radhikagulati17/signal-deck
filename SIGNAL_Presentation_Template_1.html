What SIGNAL changes, at a glance

Final pitches become defended recommendations. Team rankings become independent viability assessment. No AI record becomes a required AI audit log. Slide decks are prohibited in favour of written reports. The full brief upfront becomes partially concealed until the event starts. 24 hours becomes 48, with a deliberate overnight rest before final judging.

Wellbeing, built in not bolted on

Mentor check-ins are framed around wellbeing, not just deliverables: understand the problem, sanity-check direction, stress-test assumptions, then a communication-only pass before final pitches. A scheduled outdoor walk and a hard rule of no mandatory work or mentor sessions after the evening wind-down protect rest before judging.
